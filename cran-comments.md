## Package purpose

Provides pre-built datasets and lookup functions for USDA Rural-Urban Continuum Codes (RUCC 2023) and Rural-Urban Commuting Area codes (RUCA 2020) for all U.S. counties and ZIP codes, along with a composite rurality score. Designed to eliminate the need for researchers to manually download and reshape USDA spreadsheets.
